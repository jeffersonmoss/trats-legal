Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Trats ("Trats," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Trats application and our text-messaging (SMS/MMS) program. By using Trats or by providing your phone number to a business that uses Trats, you agree to the practices described here.

Who We Are

Trats is a bilingual (English/Spanish) job-management and customer-communication tool used by field-service businesses — such as roofing, painting, cleaning, landscaping, and catering companies — to manage jobs and to communicate with their own customers about those jobs.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you or a business provide, such as name, phone number, email address, and service address.
  • Job-related information, such as appointments, estimates, job status, messages, and photos related to a job.
  • Message content and delivery data for texts sent and received through the service.
  • Basic technical and usage information needed to operate and secure the application.

How We Use Information

  • To schedule, perform, and follow up on jobs and service requests.
  • To send service-related text messages, including appointment and estimate confirmations, scheduling and reschedule notices, crew-arrival updates, job updates, and replies to your questions.
  • To provide customer support and to operate, maintain, and improve the service.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Text Messaging (SMS/MMS) Program

If you provide your mobile number to a business that uses Trats, you may receive service-related text messages about your jobs. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, and you can get help by replying HELP or by contacting us using the details below.

No mobile information will be sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third party for marketing. Information is shared only with service providers strictly as needed to deliver the messages and operate the service (for example, our messaging carrier), or as required by law.

How We Share Information

We share information only:

  • With the field-service business you are working with, so it can serve you;
  • With service providers that help us operate the application and deliver messages, under confidentiality obligations and only as necessary;
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights and safety.

We do not sell personal information.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service and for legitimate business or legal purposes, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • Reply STOP to any message to stop receiving texts.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information.

Children's Privacy

Trats is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent change.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at tratshelp@jeffersonmoss.net.
